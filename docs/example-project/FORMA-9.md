# FORMA-9 — Illustrative Example Project

## Disclosure

FORMA-9 is a fictional, AI-generated teaching example. It is not a client
result, operating store, medical product, audit, or endorsement. The catalog
uses fictional compounds and has no dosage, treatment, efficacy, payment, or
fulfilment functionality.

## Concept

A premium research-peptide catalog for design-conscious laboratory buyers. The
primary illustrative conversion is requesting catalog access.

## Locked visual identity

- Mineral-black gallery and obsidian plinths.
- One smoked-glass hero vial with a pearl ceramic cap.
- One chrome-and-translucent-glass peptide chain with six nodes.
- Electric-cyan rim light and restrained ultraviolet refraction.
- Large negative space, precise composition, and luxury editorial rendering.
- No people, needles, pills, medical procedures, claims, labels, or generated
  interface text.

## Master image-generation prompt

```text
Use case: stylized-concept
Asset type: master website art-direction frame for a fictional premium
peptide-research storefront
Primary request: create the definitive visual identity for FORMA-9, centered
on one sculptural smoked-glass laboratory vial and an elegant molecular
peptide-chain sculpture orbiting it
Scene/backdrop: cavernous mineral-black studio with a glossy obsidian plinth,
faint atmospheric haze, and large areas of deep negative space
Subject: a single consistent hero vial made from dark smoked glass, pearl
ceramic cap, thin electric-cyan illuminated measurement line with no numbers,
accompanied by a chrome-and-translucent-glass peptide chain with exactly six
linked nodes
Style/medium: high-end cinematic 3D product render, luxury biotech editorial
art direction, physically plausible materials, premium animated-website key
visual
Composition/framing: wide landscape composition, slightly low three-quarter
camera, vial and molecule centered with generous breathing room
Lighting/mood: controlled cyan rim light, subtle ultraviolet refraction,
pearl-white key light, sophisticated and precise rather than loud neon
Constraints: no people; no needles; no pills; no medical procedure; no health
or efficacy implication; no dosage information; no readable text; no labels;
no logos; no watermark; one vial only; exactly six molecular nodes
```

Each Step 1–10 image was generated from that master frame as an image reference
with the identity invariants repeated. Exact artifact text and interface copy
are rendered by the app in HTML/CSS.
